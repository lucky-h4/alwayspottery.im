The potter

Hello, I'm [your name]

I've been making pottery from my home studio on the Isle of Man for over a decade. What started as a weekend class became a quiet obsession — and eventually, a practice that shapes my days.

I work mostly in stoneware and porcelain, drawn to earthy glazes and forms that feel good in the hand. I make things I'd want to use myself: mugs that feel right first thing in the morning, bowls wide enough for a proper meal, vases that hold a single flower well.

Every piece is made one at a time, in my garden studio, surrounded by the kind of quiet that lets you think.
